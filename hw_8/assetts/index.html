A human-centered design toolkit to better serve your citizens

Our Vision

Citizen-Centered Design applies approaches from Human-Centered Design to the realm of government. We have created a toolkit that can equip you as government employees with Citizen-Centered Design methods that you can use within your community to improve the delivery of government services. Whether you are helping your citizens file 311 reports more easily, connecting them with public transportation, or helping them enroll for a municpal ID, you can incorporate these simple methods into your service team's workflow to make sure that your service is meeting the needs of your community.

We are excited to help you champion innovation within government. Our toolkit is an ongoing effort, but we have started with sharing how you can use Citizen-Centered Design to improve an existing service. With each step, you will also be able to download a worksheet so that you can start using Citizen-Centered Design with your team. Scroll below to get started.
